Note Modification Notes
A
 The bearing spacer, 06-1955, needs to be shortened by .056".
B
 The bearing spacer, 06-7748, needs to be shortened by .056".
C
 The dummy axle, 06-2067, needs to be shortened by .056" and the bearing bore on the drum needs to be extended by .056"
D
 The dummy axle, 06-5552, needs to be shortened by .056" and the bearing bore on the sprocket needs to be extended by .056"
